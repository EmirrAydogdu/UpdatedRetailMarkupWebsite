We Answered for You

An Order Management System (OMS) is software that manages the entire order lifecycle across e-commerce, marketplaces, and retail stores. It centralizes sales, inventory, billing and shipping in one platform. OMS improves inventory visibility, reduces errors, and enhances customer experience by automating manual processes.

An OMS collects orders from multiple sales channels such as online stores, marketplaces (Amazon, Trendyol, Hepsiburada) and physical shops. It updates inventory in real time, generates invoices, and routes shipments to the nearest warehouse. This enables faster order fulfillment, accurate stock management and better customer satisfaction.

Yes. A professional OMS platform follows industry-level data security standards. All transactions are encrypted, hosted on secure servers and protected with access controls. Regular backups and updates ensure compliance and business continuity.

An OMS integrates with inventory management and warehouse management systems (WMS) to track stock in real time. It prevents overstocking or stockouts, automates reorders, and optimizes supply chain operations. This reduces costs while delivering faster and more accurate service to customers.

Yes. OMS supports package splitting for marketplace orders (Trendyol, Hepsiburada, N11, LC Waikiki, Amazon). Items can be shipped from different stores or warehouses, ensuring faster delivery and accurate fulfillment.

OMS can integrate with leading shipping and cargo providers such as Trendyol Express, HepsiJet, Yurtiçi, MNG and Sürat. Additional custom integrations are possible to deliver orders quickly and reliably according to your business needs.
